Why use a time‑zone planner?

Coordinating meetings across regions can be challenging because each city follows its own time zone and observes daylight saving differently. Our planner converts your local meeting time to other major cities, helping you schedule calls without confusion. To maximise business productivity:

  • Check the overlap between working hours in different regions.
  • Consider daylight saving changes in spring and autumn.
  • Send calendar invites that include the time zone for clarity.
